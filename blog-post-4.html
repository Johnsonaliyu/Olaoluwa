Olaoluwa Medical Outreach Brings Free Healthcare to Underserved Community Members

üìÖ July 30, 2025

Olaoluwa Age Group has once again demonstrated its commitment to community welfare through a successful medical outreach programme aimed at providing free healthcare services to underserved members of the community. The initiative was designed to improve access to basic medical care and promote early detection of common health conditions.

During the outreach, beneficiaries received free blood pressure checks and blood sugar tests, alongside medical consultations. Qualified health personnel also prescribed medications where necessary, with drugs provided at no cost to participants. The programme attracted a large turnout of residents, particularly elderly persons and individuals who rarely have access to routine medical care.

Olaoluwa Age Group reaffirmed its commitment to sustaining impactful social interventions that uplift lives and strengthen community development.
