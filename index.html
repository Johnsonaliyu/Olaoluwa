Who We Are?

Rooted in culture, driven by purpose

Olaoluwa is a collection of youths of Owalusin descent and long-time residents who were born, raised, or educated within the community.

We are deeply rooted in the spatial, cultural, and traditional values of Owalusin, not as outsiders but as true stakeholders. United by shared goals in community development, social interaction, and integration, we work together for progress. Our Programs

Creating lasting impact in our community

Spelling Bee

Our Spelling Bee program encourages academic excellence and healthy competition among students. We provide a platform for young minds to showcase their spelling skills while building confidence and fostering intellectual growth.

Medical Outreach

We are committed to improving community health through our Medical Outreach initiatives. Our program brings quality healthcare services and health education to underserved members of the Owalusin community.

Street Games

Street Games promotes social bonding and community spirit. Through organized recreational activities, we bring together members of all ages for fun, health, and lasting friendships.
