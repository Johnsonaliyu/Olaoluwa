Neigbhourhood Watch: Olaoluwa tasked with the security of Owalusin

üìÖ January 5, 2026

In response to the growing wave of insecurity across the country, community leaders in Owalusin have mandated Olaoluwa Age Group, alongside other age groups, to actively safeguard the community through a coordinated neighbourhood watch initiative.

The decision was reached following consultations aimed at strengthening local security and ensuring the safety of lives and property within Owalusin. The initiative emphasizes collective responsibility, vigilance, and collaboration among residents.

Speaking on the development, the Chairman of the Olaoluwa Age Group, Mr. Shittu Funsho, urged members to demonstrate commitment by turning up consistently for night watch duties. He stressed that community security requires unity, discipline, and proactive participation.

The Olaoluwa Age Group reaffirmed its readiness to support the mandate and work closely with other stakeholders to maintain peace and security in Owalusin.
