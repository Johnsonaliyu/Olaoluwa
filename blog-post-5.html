Olaoluwa Hosts Preliminary Spelling Bee Rounds for Schools Across Iwaro-Oka

üìÖ July 19, 2025

Olaoluwa Age Group successfully hosted the preliminary rounds of its Spelling Bee competition for schools across Iwaro-Oka, reaffirming its commitment to promoting literacy and academic excellence among young learners. The event was held at Owalusin Community Primary School and attracted wide participation from schools within the axis.

Over 40 schools competed in the preliminary rounds, with pupils showcasing impressive spelling skills and academic confidence in a highly competitive and engaging atmosphere. The event provided a platform for pupils to demonstrate their spelling prowess while encouraging healthy academic rivalry among participating schools.

In his welcome address, the Secretary of Olaoluwa Age Group, Mr. Ehinola Oluwafemi, warmly welcomed the pupils and teachers, appreciating them for showing up and trusting the process of developing the academic potential of their pupils. He further encouraged the pupils to be bold, courageous, and confident as they competed, reminding them that participation itself is a step towards personal growth and excellence.
